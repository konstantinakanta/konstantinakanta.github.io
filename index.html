About

I'm a Research Scientist at Beiersdorf in Hamburg, working across the full data-science stack: from raw sensor measurements and biological data, through statistical modeling and machine learning, to production deployment on Azure.

My work spans time-series analysis, computer vision, NLP, and MLOps. I build and maintain end-to-end pipelines — from data ingestion and preprocessing to model deployment and monitoring.

Before Beiersdorf, I was a Data Scientist at Shutterstock in Dublin (search & ranking), and a Cloud Integration Engineer at Ericsson. I hold an MSc in Computer Science from University College Dublin and an MSc in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the Democritus University of Thrace.

Projects

Research

Pneumonia Detection from Chest X-rays

Compared four deep-learning architectures (logistic regression baseline, LeNet-5, custom CNN with data augmentation, VGG16) for binary pneumonia classification on chest X-ray images. Transfer learning from ImageNet with VGG16 performed best. Implemented in Python with scikit-learn, OpenCV, Keras, and PyTorch.

Research

Fusing Rankings in Information Retrieval with Voting Systems

Diploma thesis at DUTh. Evaluated voting-system approaches to rank fusion (Borda, RRF, Condorcet, CombSUM/MNZ) on standard IR test collections using TREC Eval. Reciprocal rank fusion proved consistently strongest — the same method later drove a 10% accuracy gain at Shutterstock.

Code

Multi-label Classifier from Scratch

From-scratch implementation of Binary Relevance (with optional under-sampling) and Classifier Chains for multi-label classification. Built to internalise every design decision — thresholding, imbalance handling, label-correlation effects — rather than relying on library defaults.

Code · Coming soon

rrf — Reciprocal Rank Fusion library

A small open-source Python package implementing RRF and related fusion methods (Borda, CombSUM, CombMNZ), plus a reproducible benchmark notebook on a public IR dataset. Motivated by RRF's recurring role in my work — from my thesis through Shutterstock to modern RAG pipelines.
